Menu

Our menu is carefully curated to tantalize your taste buds and transport you on a culinary journey like no other. From exquisite appetizers to mouthwatering main courses and decadent desserts.

Appetizers

  • Crispy Calamari

    Tender calamari rings lightly breaded and fried to perfection. Served with a zesty marinara dipping sauce.

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh buffalo mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, and basil leaves drizzled with balsamic glaze and extra virgin olive oil.

  • Garlic Parmesan Chicken Wings

    Succulent chicken wings tossed in a savory garlic parmesan sauce, served with celery sticks and ranch dressing.

Main Course

  • Grilled Ribeye Steak

    Juicy ribeye steak seasoned with our special blend of spices, grilled to perfection, and served with garlic mashed potatoes and seasonal vegetables.

  • Vegetable Pad Thai

    Stir-fried rice noodles with tofu, bean sprouts, carrots, bell peppers, and green onions in a tangy tamarind sauce, garnished with crushed peanuts and cilantro.

  • Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken

    Tender chicken breast marinated in lemon and herbs, roasted to golden perfection, served with wild rice pilaf and steamed asparagus.

  • Shrimp Scampi Pasta

    Succulent shrimp sautéed with garlic, white wine, and lemon butter sauce, tossed with linguine pasta and finished with fresh parsley.

Desserts

  • Classic New York Cheesecake

    Creamy cheesecake on a graham cracker crust, topped with your choice of raspberry or strawberry coulis.

  • Chocolate Lava Cake

    Decadent chocolate cake with a molten chocolate center, served warm with vanilla bean ice cream and drizzled with chocolate sauce.

  • Tiramisu

    Layers of espresso-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cheese, dusted with cocoa powder and served with a dollop of whipped cream.

  • Crème Brûlée

    Rich custard topped with a caramelized sugar crust, served with fresh berries and a delicate almond biscotti.
